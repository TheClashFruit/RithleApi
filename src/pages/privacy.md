title: Privacy Policy
---

**Effective Date:** Jun 1, 2026

At Rithle, we believe in keeping your data private and minimal. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is kept secure by the developer, **TheClashFruit**.

### 1. Data We Collect

We only collect the absolute minimum amount of data required to run the application, provide user features, and fix technical bugs.

#### A. Account Data (Tied to your Modrinth Profile)

- **Modrinth User ID:** Collected securely via Modrinth OAuth when you log in. TheClashFruit does not see or store your Modrinth password.
- **Registration Time:** The exact date and time you first logged into Rithle.
- **Last Opened Time:** The date and time of the last time you opened the app. Only the _most recent_ timestamp is kept; previous open times are overwritten.
- **Donation Status / Badge Info:** If you choose to donate to support the project, a record of your donation status is tied to your Modrinth User ID so TheClashFruit can award you a special donation badge within the app.

#### B. Technical Data (Completely Anonymous)

- **Anonymized Crash Logs:** If the application crashes, a log containing technical details about the error (such as the line of code that failed, application version, and operating system) is sent to us. These logs are entirely anonymized, meaning they do not contain your Modrinth ID, username, or any personal identifying information.

### 2. How We Use Your Data

TheClashFruit uses this data solely to:

- Manage your account and display earned donation badges within Rithle.
- Identify, diagnose, and fix software bugs or stability issues using the anonymous crash logs.

Your data will **never** be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

### 3. Data Retention and Automatic Deletion

We don't hold onto your account data if you aren't using the app:

- **1-Year Inactivity Deletion:** If you do not open Rithle for a continuous period of **one (1) year**, your Modrinth User ID, registration time, donation badge status, and last opened time will be automatically and permanently deleted from the database.
- **Crash Log Retention:** Anonymous crash logs are kept only as long as necessary to diagnose and deploy a fix for the bug, after which they are cleared.

### 4. Security

Reasonable technical security measures are taken to protect your data within the database. Because Rithle authentication relies on Modrinth OAuth, your account security is directly tied to your Modrinth account.

### 5. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data stored about you or to request manual deletion of your account before the 1-year automatic deletion period. Please note that deleting your account data will also remove any donation badges tied to your Modrinth User ID. (Because crash logs are entirely anonymous, we cannot look up or delete specific crash data on request).

### 6. Changes to This Policy

If the way data is handled changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

### 7. Contact

If you have any questions, want to request manual deletion of your data, or need to inquire about donation badges, you can reach out directly to **TheClashFruit** at:

- **Email:** admin@theclashfruit.me
- **Web:** https://rthl.theclashfruit.me/opt-out
